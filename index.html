Our Engineering Process

How we bring complex mechatronic ideas from napkin sketches to fully functional, deployment-ready hardware solutions.

01

Concept & CAD Design

We transform your specifications into detailed 3D CAD assemblies using SolidWorks, ensuring precise mechanical alignment and parts clearance.

02

FEA & Multiphysics

We simulate structural stresses, thermal loads, and fluid behavior in ANSYS & COMSOL to optimize weight and ensure safety before fabrication.

03

Electronics & PCB

We design custom electrical schematics, select components for efficiency, and route robust multilayer PCBs using Altium or KiCad.

04

Firmware & Edge AI

We write efficient C/C++ firmware for ESP32/STM32 microcontrollers, integrate sensors, and deploy Edge AI models (TinyML) directly on-device.

05

Assembly & Delivery

We machine parts, assemble physical enclosures, perform system calibration, and deliver a fully-tested mechatronics product ready for operation.
